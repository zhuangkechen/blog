新的开始

2016-12-14

Nanjing is a indescribable city, with come historical old and enigmatical desolation. I never fell in love with this city, yet still live in it for last 10 years. Some said 10 years is a kind of ridge, you need to make some change. luckly, my change finally gonna coming.

What about a new life and new life style

So I start my new blog, and plan to get the PhD soon and leaving Nanjing to south..

in a $x_i = new^{fun}$ math way

$$future(me) = \begin{cases} \sum_{i=1}^{n}\prod_\epsilon(get\_PhD) & ; x=first \\ \int_{1y}^{Ny}work\_hard & ; x=second \\ Marry(me \bigcup my\_love)\longrightarrow settle\_down\coprod & ; x=third \\ more\_yet\_to\_come & ; x\in(4,\infty) \\ \cdots \end{cases}$$

Wish we all have a bright future！
